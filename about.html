Driven by a profound belief in the transformative power of technology, Ogu Princewill has cultivated a dynamic career in IT and engineering. Holding a Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering from Michael Okpara University of Agriculture, Umudike, he has demonstrated expertise in various technical and leadership roles. Currently serving as an ICT and Coding Instructor at Claret International, Princewill educates learners on coding and ICT, manages hardware installations, and oversees database administration.
Previously a staff at Platinum Power Planet Ltd, where he installed and supervised solar systems installations. During their National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) tenure, Princewill received commendation for outstanding leadership as the Overall General Parade Commander and Platoon Leader. As a Corps member, he also taught welding and fabrication at Government Technical College, Ika, Akwa Ibom State.
With His proficiency in JavaScript, cyber security, and report writing, along with strong leadership and communication skills, Princewill is dedicated to leveraging technology for positive change and contributing to the success of innovative organizations.
